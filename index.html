Investment Growth Calculator

Welcome to the Investment Growth Calculator!

This tool allows you to calculate how much your investment will grow by the time you reach retirement. Simply enter the following details:

  • Initial Investment: The amount of money you plan to invest (e.g., $5,000).
  • Annual Interest Rate: The percentage rate at which your investment will grow each year (e.g., 7%).
  • Year of Retirement: The year you plan to retire (e.g., 2040).

Once you click "Calculate", the tool will apply the compound interest formula to determine how much your investment will be worth by the time you retire. The result will be displayed in a complete sentence, and it will round the value to the nearest whole number for easier understanding.
